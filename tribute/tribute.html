Satoru Iwata: The Unforgotten Preisdent & CEO of Nintendo

“Above all, video games are meant to just be one thing: Fun for everyone.” - Satoru Iwata

Satoru Iwata was born on Dec 6. 1959. During his youth, Iwata was known as a leader of many different school activites from middle & high school. During his Junior year, however, Iwata started developing games. Many games that he developed caught on to people who invited Iwata to their company to develop games for them. Not long after, he became the 4th president of Nintendo eventually leading up to him becoming CEO.

Developed two new systems; the Nintendo DS and the Nintendo Wii, Nintendo DS also lead to the development of the also successful DS Lite and DSi, Became the fourth Nintendo President, Was in the top 30 CEO's worldwide (2007-2009), and Worked with HAL Laboratory before Nintendo.

Hokkaido Sapporo South High School & Assisted subsidiary's head engineer: Yash Terakura
